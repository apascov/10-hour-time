1 custom second = 0.864 Earth seconds

This is an artwork in the scope of the presentism movement, it finally makes time convenient for a human use - our system of 100 seconds per minute, 100 minutes per hour and 10 hours per day, allows you to escape the obsolete 60-digit system. Now, the day is divided in 10 000 equal parts, each as long as the human cardiac cycle (heartbeat). The communication becomes efficient: half an hour = 50 min, quarter = 25 min, etc. The calculations become seamless: how many seconds in 27 min = 2700 sec. Hopefully, due to our invention even time can become perfectly square.
